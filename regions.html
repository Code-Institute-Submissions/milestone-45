Kent

Famously known as the ‘Garden of England’, the english county of Kent also lays claim to some of the countries leading wine producers with many wines punching well above their weight in national and international markets.

Sussex

With over 700 hectares of vineyards, Sussex has the highest cocentration of vines in the UK! Classified as having a "maritime" climate, the region is one of the sunniest (and warmest) in England helps to produce some spectacuar wines.

Essex

Not as well known a wine producing region as Kent but is starting to produce some top rated bottles. With 14 producers (at the moment!), it is a region to watch out for in the next few years!

Hampshire

Hampshire wineries are growing from strength to strength and produce high quality sparkling and white wine. The quality of the wine is helped by the warm climate and disinctive, chalky terrain - very similar to that found in Champagne.
