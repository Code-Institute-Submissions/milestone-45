Red Wine

The main varietals, successfully, grown in England are Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier.

2019 Borge Red

Cherry red, with aromas of red berries, casis, and mulberries. Soft tannins and perfectly balanced acidity creates a wine redolent of English summer fruits.

2018 Red Pinot

Pale cherry red with a vibrant translucency the wine displays raspberry and clove characters on the nose. The palate is light, elegant and rich, being underpinned by an earthy spiciness and a long finish.

The Red Miller 2018

Made exclusively from beautifully ripe Pinot Meunier, the ‘quiet partner’ in classic sparkling blends. A short three-day fermentation on the skins, followed by a brief time in French and American oak.

Balfour Luke's Pinot Noir 2018

Named after the Producers’ eldest son. This wine is made from specially selected Burgundian clones and is lightly oaked using French and American barrels, imparting soft but firm tannins.




White Wine

The main varietals, successfully, grown in England are Chardonnay, Bacchus and Seyval Blanc.

Bacchus Reserve 2017

This limited production wine has intense aromas of gooseberry, nettle and melon. Tropical fruits are distinctive on the palate and balanced acidity leads to a long finish.

Bacchus 2018

A crisp, refreshing highly aromatic white wine characterised by melon and peach on the nose. The palate is well balanced and has a light and delicate finish.

Balfour's Skye Blend 2018

Named after the Producer’s eldest daughter; Skye, this wine is an exciting blend of Bacchus, Chardonnay and Pinot Blanc. This wine is aromatic, crisp and intensely fruity.

2014 Element 20

Complex, mature, elegant and stylish, this silky, rich, full bodied wine has a balancing acidity that will ensure that even further complexity is achieved in years to follow.




Sparkling Wine

The majority of vines planted in recent years have been the traditional champagne varieties of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier, which in total represent more than 60% of all vines planted in the UK (Wine Standards official figures for 2018).

Most sparkling wines are made using grapes harvested in a single year (vintage) but some non-vintage (NV) are also available. Classic Cuvée designates that the wine has been made from the traditional champagne grape varieties of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and Meunier. Blanc de Blancs indicates that only white grapes have been used, often exclusively Chardonnay.

Chapel Down Rose Brut

A delicate rosé with aromas of ripe strawberries and raspberries with background notes of toasty shortbread. The palate is focused and pure contributing to the crisp and fresh style.

Nyetimber Classic Cuvee

The signature wine of Nyetimber is a classic blend of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, and Pinot Meunier and is a perfect balance of elegance and intensity.

Balfour Brut Rose 2016

Hush Heath’s iconic sparkling rosé is made in small quantities at our state of the art winery in deepest Kent. Palest pink with crisp English acidity, rich fruit and a refreshingly long finish.

Chapel Down Brut NV

An elegant English sparkling wine with aromas of red apple, lemongrass and freshly baked bread together with hints of strawberry and quince on the palate and fine persistent bubbles.
