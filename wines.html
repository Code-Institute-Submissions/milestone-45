Red Wine

The main varietals, successfully, grown in England are Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier.

The Red Miller 2018

Made exclusively from beautifully ripe Pinot Meunier, usually the ‘quiet partner’ in classic sparkling blends, this distinctive wine is certainly a first for England. A short three-day fermentation on the skins, was followed by a brief time in French and American oak. With minimal filtration, the result is a delicate and aromatic red wine that is near impossible to replicate.

Balfour Luke's Pinot Noir 2018

This Pinot Noir is made from specially selected Burgundian clones at the Hush Heath Estate and is lightly oaked using French and American barrels, imparting soft but firm tannins. Expect typical cool-climate Pinot Noir characters. Gold Medal winner at the Global Pinot Noir Masters.

No'4 Syrah 2018 by Domaine Gayda

A classic style with aromas of cooked dark berries and plums finished with cedar on the nose. The palate is rich with well-integrated oak and fine tannins leading to a long, smooth finish. The team at Domaine Gayda are focused on a quest for quality and innovation and are delighted to be partnering with Chapel Down. The winemaking process encompasses some very traditional elements such as a pre-fermentation cold-soak, indigenous alcoholic fermentation and an extended post-fermentation maceration.

Nyetimber

2018 Litmus Red Pinot is made from 100% Pinot Noir that was cold soaked prior to fermentation on its skins and spent 19 months in one to five-year-old French oak barriques prior to bottling. Pale cherry red with a vibrant translucency the wine displays raspberry and clove characters on the nose. The palate is light, elegant and rich, being underpinned by an earthy spiciness and a long finish. This wine will gain further complexity with further cellaring.

White Wine

The main varietals, successfully, grown in England are Chardonnay, Bacchus and Seyval Blanc.

Chapel Down Bacchus 2018

A great example of a quality English wine white and from a grape variety that is becoming regarded as the still wine varietal of England. A crisp, refreshing highly aromatic white wine characterised by melon and peach on the nose. The palate is well balanced and has a light and delicate finish.

Balfour Skye's Blend 2018

Named after the Producer’s eldest daughter; Skye, this wine is an exciting blend of Bacchus, Chardonnay and Pinot Blanc from the exceptional 2018 harvest. This wine is aromatic, crisp and intensely fruity.

2014 Element 20

The 2014 growing season was sensational. Perfect flowering conditions followed by August rains and a warm September led to early ripening of a good crop in the coolness of early October. The 2014 Element 20 departs from the usual blend of Bacchus and Chardonnay consisting of 35% Chardonnay, 35% Pinot Gris and 30% Pinot Blanc. Fermented separately in one to five-year-old French oak barriques the wine was left on full lees for eleven months before bottling. Complex, mature, elegant and stylish, this silky, rich, full bodied wine has a balancing acidity that will ensure that even further complexity is achieved in years to follow.

Bacchus Reserve 2017

This limited production wine has intense aromas of gooseberry, nettle and melon. Tropical fruits are distinctive on the palate and balanced acidity leads to a long finish.

Sparkling Wine

The majority of vines planted in recent years have been the traditional champagne varieties of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier, which in total represent more than 60% of all vines planted in the UK (Wine Standards official figures for 2018).

Most sparkling wines are made using grapes harvested in a single year (vintage) but some non-vintage (NV) are also available. Classic Cuvée designates that the wine has been made from the traditional champagne grape varieties of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and Meunier. Blanc de Blancs indicates that only white grapes have been used, often exclusively Chardonnay.

Nyetimber Classic Cuvee

The signature wine of Nyetimber is a classic blend of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, and Pinot Meunier and is a perfect balance of elegance and intensity. The blend includes wines from all our vineyards and also from several vintages.

Balfour Brut Rose 2016

Balfour Brut Rosé is made from the three classic grape varieties- Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier. This single vineyard wine is made using selected parcels of the finest grapes from the oldest vineyard at Hush Heath Estate, Oast House Meadow. Hush Heath’s iconic sparkling rosé is made in small quantities at our state of the art winery in deepest Kent, the Garden of England. Palest pink with crisp English acidity, rich fruit and a refreshingly long finish. Uniquely Balfour.

Chapel Down Rose Brut

A delicate rosé with aromas of ripe strawberries and raspberries with background notes of toasty shortbread. The palate is focused and pure, its fine mousse contributing to the crisp and fresh style.

Chapel Down Brut NV

A great example of a classic English sparkling wine. An elegant English sparkling wine with aromas of red apple, lemongrass and freshly baked bread together with hints of strawberry and quince on the palate and fine persistent bubbles.
